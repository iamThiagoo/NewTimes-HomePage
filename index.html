What is "New Times"?

We are a clothing company created in November 2003 by Thiago Leonard. In a few months of existence, Thiago L.'s company already had 100 employees, including experienced designers in the market. Today, the New Times Corporation is present in 7 countries and has 20 headquarters around the world ... and growing.

Plans for this year

This year's New Times plans are unmissable. Starting with the summer collection that is ready to be launched in July. And the winter collection is already in the final part, and is expected to be launched in December. But not only in fashion does the New Times survive, this year it is planned to open its headquarters and stores in Benfica/ Portugal, Rio de Janeiro/ Brazil and in Bayern/ Germany. And more news will come ... we just can't count them so as not to spoil the surprise.
